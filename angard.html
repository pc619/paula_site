Summary

Brief

To create a CAD model of lightweight tandem bike CAD model with higher natural frequency than 30Hz and minimum product life of 10 years. Conduct FEA analysis to test.

To achieve the brief above, I first made a solid model of a tandem bike on solidworks. Then, I identified the methods for the FEA simulations (i.e., boundary and loading conditions, simulation parameters, etc.). This model was then analysed using two different materials (Aluminum Alloy 7075 â€“ T6, and Titanium Ti-5Al-2.5Sn). Sanity checks and mesh refinement were also conducted.

In order to improve the natural frequency of the bike, a new solidworks model was made. This revised model was analyzed using the same methods and the results were compared.
