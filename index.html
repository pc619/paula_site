About me

Hello! I am Paula Castillero Garcia, a Spanish 21 year-old fourth year Design Engineering student at Imperial.

Throughout my life I've been lucky to live in different places. I grew up in Madrid until I was 10 years old. Then I moved to Dubai, where I lived until I graduated from high school in 2019. Now I am enjoying London during my higher education journey!

During my time in Dubai and London, I've been exposed to an international and innovative environment. I have developed an interest in confronting and resolving new challenges, dealing with ambiguity and applying problem solving approaches. Moreover, I have grown up sorrounded by diverse cultures, acknowledging the importance of nurturing knowledge and constant learning. I look forward to integrating myself in multicultural teams to solve interdisplinary challenges.
