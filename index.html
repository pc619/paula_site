About me

Hello! My name is Paula Castillero Garcia. I am a spanish 20 year-old currently studying third year Design Engineering at Imperial.

Throughout my life I've moved around quite a bit. I lived in Madrid until I was 10 years old. Then, I moved to Dubai, where I lived until I graduated from high school in 2019. Now, I am in London for my higher education journey!

During my time in Dubai, I was exposed to an international and innovative world. This made me develop a passion for the complexity, creativity, and unpredictability of tech (especially that which is designed for people). I am now excited to begin my professional journey in this sector and be able to contribute to its progress.
