About me

Hello! My name is Paula Castillero Garcia, I am a spanish 19 year-old currently studying second year Design Enginneering at Imperial.

In the academic spectrum, I love designing both physical and digital products and using computing and engineering skills to make the designs a reality. But, I also love horse riding, travelling and hiking!
