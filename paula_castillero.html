About me

Hello! My name is Paula Castillero Garcia, I am a spanish 19 year-old currently studying second year Design Enginneering at Imperial.

In the academic spectrum, I love designing both physical and digital products and using computing and engineering skills to make the designs a reality. But, I also love horse riding, travelling and hiking!


Hello world!

I was born in Spain in 2001, where I lived for 10 years. I'll always love the great, great food that my country has to offer and the beautiful family culture.

Bye, dad :(

In 2011 my dad moved to Dubai on his own, it was hard to be separated from my family for the first time.

Hello Dubai!

Then it was our turn to join him. It was a challenge to leave my family behind and I didn't even speak english.

High school done

Ready to go to uni in a new country... which would never have happened if we never moved to Dubai. In Spain, I just wanted to be close to family, but in Dubai you learn to seek opportunities all over the world.

From Dubai, to London

And now, here I am, studying Design Engineering at Imperial!
